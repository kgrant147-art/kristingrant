Venmo × Taco Bell

Brand Campaign Creator Strategy Social PR Shorty Gold Webby Nominee
+

"What's the wildest thing you'd do for a free taco?"

A full-funnel social and creator campaign that embedded Venmo inside a genuine cultural moment rather than advertising around one. The insight: the great lengths people go to for free food is universally funny — and paying with Venmo in the Taco Bell app is infinitely easier than any of them. The Venmo Taco Bellt — a limited-edition leather taco holster handcrafted by the artisan behind Kendrick Lamar's custom pieces — became the campaign's hero moment, covered by 50+ national outlets and driving the highest Tuesday Drop entries in Taco Bell history.

Zillow for Warcraft

D&AD Graphite Pencil Brand × Gaming Microsite Creator Partnership Earned Media
+

"What if you could tour Thrall's home on Zillow?"

The question nobody expected a real estate platform to answer. A three-phase brand partnership that took Zillow's core product — home browsing — and placed it inside the world's most iconic fantasy universe, earning press coverage in gaming, lifestyle, and mainstream media that no media buy could have generated. Phase 1: Zillow for Azeroth microsite with Showcase 3D tours of WoW character homes and in-game reward mechanic. Phase 2: Sho'kase Neighborhood — WoW creators listing their in-game homes on Zillow. Phase 3: Blizzcon IRL activation.

Silicon Valley Bank — "Yes, SVB."

Brand Rebuild OOH & DOOH Comms Strategy Reputation Recovery Drum Awards Entry
+

"Reputation once lost isn't gone forever."

After the third-largest bank failure in US history, SVB faced the most daunting brand challenge in modern finance: convincing the innovation economy that it had not only survived, but was the same unwavering partner it had always been. There was no playbook. We wrote one. Under the platform "Relentless Partners in Possibilities," the "Yes, SVB." campaign used bold OOH and DOOH placements across Times Square, CalTrain stations in SF, and guerrilla advertising at NY Tech Week — with AI-designed creative featuring striking colors and wave-like patterns — to reassert SVB's voice in the innovation economy.
